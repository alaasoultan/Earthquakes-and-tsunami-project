# Earthquakes-and-tsunami-project
This is a project analyzing the data of the earthquakes that was recorded from 2001 to 2022 and whether each one of them was accompanied with a tsunami or not. having the location of the earthquake using it's latitude and longitude on the map, it's magnitude, it's significance, how much did the people feel it, it's strength near the earth's surface , how many stations recorded it, the distance between the earthquake center and the nearest station that recorded it and the largest horizontal distance between the stations that recorded it.
